Search posted Jobs

NC Nonprofit Careers serves as an access point and one-stop site for nonprofit employers, job seekers, and individuals looking for internship opportunities. Nonprofit employers can post job openings and internship opportunities. Job seekers can post their résumés and make them available to nonprofit organizations across North Carolina. It's cost-effective, efficient, and targeted specifically to nonprofits and job seekers in North Carolina.
